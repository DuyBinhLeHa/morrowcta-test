About MCTA

Discover Morrow: Where Every Season Brings a Celebration

Morrow's events are thoughtfully crafted to strengthen community bonds, providing ample opportunities for individuals to celebrate, learn, and experience the unique culture of our city.

10,000+ attendees
for big events
Connecting
communities

Founded in response to the growing need for a dedicated organization to promote tourism in Morrow, Georgia, MCTA has been committed from the outset to enhancing the visitor experience. By offering valuable resources, curating impactful events, and highlighting local attractions, MCTA works to connect travelers with the vibrant cultural offerings of our community. Over time, we have nurtured essential partnerships and continuously evolved to meet the ever-changing needs of tourists.
