The District

1065 Olde Towne Morrow Rd, Morrow, GA 30260

Located just south of Atlanta and only 15 minutes from Hartsfield-Jackson Atlanta International Airport, The District is a dynamic mixed-use development that blends historic charm with modern amenities. Showcasing beautifully preserved 19th-century homes alongside boutiques, eateries, and event spaces, it also serves as one of the region’s premier outdoor event venues.
The District offers a wide range of vibrant, family-friendly activities and hosts a variety of community events, including Lunar New Year, Juneteenth, Day of the Dead, International Night Market, and seasonal festivities. With ongoing growth and a strong commitment to innovation, The District stands as a proud symbol of the City of Morrow’s dedication to community development and cultural enrichment.
